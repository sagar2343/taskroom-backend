Features

Everything you need to run field teams

Built for managers who need accountability, not just scheduling software.

📍

Live GPS Tracking

Employees' GPS pings every 30 seconds. View live location and full route traces from the manager dashboard.

Real-time
📋

Multi-Step Tasks

Break tasks into ordered steps with individual deadlines. Each step can require photo proof, signatures, or location check-ins.

📸

Photo Proof

Require employees to capture photos at each step. Evidence is stored and visible to managers in real-time.

✍️

Digital Signatures

Collect customer or supervisor signatures digitally. Signature images are attached to task completion records.

🏠

Room-Based Teams

Organize employees into rooms by project, region, or department. Managers control who's in each room.

📊

Manager Dashboard

See all active tasks, employee status, completion rates, and today's activity at a glance. No guesswork.

🎯

Destination Check

Set physical destinations for field steps. The app verifies the employee has actually arrived at the correct location.

⚡

Priority & Deadlines

Assign low/medium/high priority. Overdue and missed tasks are flagged instantly so nothing falls through the cracks.

📱

Mobile-First Employee App

Employees work from the Task Room mobile app. Managers get full visibility from this web dashboard.

iOS + Android
