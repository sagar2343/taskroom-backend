Features

Everything your field team needs

Built for managers who need accountability and employees who need clarity.

📍

Live GPS Tracking

Track your field team in real time on a live map. WebSocket-powered updates every few seconds — no polling, no delays.

✅

Step-by-Step Tasks

Break tasks into ordered steps with deadlines, destinations, and validations. Employees follow a clear workflow every time.

📷

Proof Collection

Require photo proof, digital signatures, and GPS location on task completion. Every step is fully auditable.

🗺

Destination Check

Pin a destination on the map. Employee must physically arrive within a defined radius before completing the step.

📊

Manager Dashboard

See all active tasks, employee locations, step progress, and completion evidence from one unified view.

🏢

Multi-Tenant Rooms

Organize your workforce into rooms. Each room has its own tasks, members, and managers with full isolation.

🔋

Background Tracking

Location updates continue when the app is in background or screen is off — powered by a foreground service.

✍

Digital Signatures

Collect signatures from customers, supervisors, or managers directly on mobile as proof of service delivery.

📈

Route History

View the complete GPS path an employee travelled. Every 20-second ping is stored and visualized on a live map.

How it works

From setup to field in minutes

Get your organization running in four simple steps.

1

Create Organization

Register here and get a unique organization code instantly — completely free.

2

Add Your Team

Share the org code. Managers and employees register in the Task Room mobile app using it.

3

Create Rooms & Tasks

Managers create rooms, assign members, then create tasks with steps and destination pins.

4

Track & Monitor

Watch your team live. View positions, task progress, and all submitted proof in real time.
